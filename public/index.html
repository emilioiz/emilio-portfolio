Credibility Statement

Emilio Izquierdo has dedicated the last eight years of his professional career to helping people and organizations do data better. Whether in an internal or external asset role, he has the proven ability to understand how organizations intake, integrate and process data. His understanding of organizational data strategy enables his ability to effectively identify gaps and opportunities of business intelligence.

As a veteran of the U.S. Army, Emilio began his career integrating complex avionics systems in special warfare helicopters. This experience sparked his passion for data. After receiving his Bachelor’s degree in Finance from the University of Maryland University College, Emilio began working in the National Institutes of Health (NIH) where, as a member of the prestigious Management Intern program, he focused on emerging technologies and data strategy. As a Management Analyst, Emilio supported the mission of the NIH across 27 institutes. Specifically, he played a critical role in the engineering and delivery of data pipelines across all NIH institutes. The pipelines Emilio engineered enabled access to clean, structured data for administrative and financial reporting and analysis.

Following his time at the NIH, Emilio joined Deloitte, where he observed similar challenges in data integration across both private and federal organizations. Being passionate about having an impact, Emilio decided that his unique professional experiences could be utilized by a broader and more diverse group of companies. This led him to found Daasly, a data strategy consulting firm, in 2015. At Daasly, Emilio serves as the Lead Data Strategy Consultant, a role in which clients have praised him for “knocking out some of the hairiest, longstanding BI challenges in innovative and value-creating ways.”

Emilio works cross-functionally, directing teams in industry-leading organizations across several industries to develop self-sufficient data teams and find ways to improve the people, process and technologies driving their business. Emilio has worked with companies across industries including home security, uniform services, life insurance, and others.

Emilio has led teams through intricate projects, many requiring quick onboarding to a company’s datasets and processes. He and his team have a proven track record for delivering excellent assessments and insights, as well as executing changes that help shorten companies’ data maturity curves. He is “the swiss army knife of data” because he is versatile, easy to work with, and sharp. Emilio brings a wealth of experience to any team or project.
